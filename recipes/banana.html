Mama's Banana Pudding 🍌


Description 💭

Dolly Parton and her sister Rachel Parton Geroge grew up with their mama, grandmas, and aunts making this classic dessert from scratch.

Ingredients 🥣

  • 1 cup plus 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 9 ripe bananas
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup flour

Directions 🍰

  1. Stir together 1 cup sugar, the flour, and salt in a large bowl. Whisk in milk, egg yolks, and whole eggs until well combined and smooth.
  2. Peel and slice bananas. Line the bottom and sides of 2 (9x13-inch) broiler-safe casserole dishes with vanilla wafers.
  3. For meringue, in a large clean bowl, using an electric mixer or a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment, beat egg whites, cream of tartar, and the remaining 1 tablespoon sugar.
  4. Set oven rack about 4 inches from broiler; turn broiler to high. Quickly broil meringues until browned, about 1 to 2 minutes.
